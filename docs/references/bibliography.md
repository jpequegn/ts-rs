### Classic Textbooks

**Hamilton, J. D. (1994)**
*Time Series Analysis*
Princeton University Press
**ISBN**: 978-0691042893
**Note**: Comprehensive mathematical treatment of time series econometrics.

*Introduction to Time Series and Forecasting* (3rd Edition)
